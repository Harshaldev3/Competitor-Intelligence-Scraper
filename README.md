# Competitor-Intelligence-Scraper
A Python-based competitor intelligence scraper that analyzes business websites and generates actionable insights for local businesses.

Competitor Intelligence Scraper

Competitor Intelligence Scraper is a Python-based business intelligence tool designed to help local businesses analyze their competitors' online presence.

The application collects publicly available information from competitor websites, extracts key business details such as services, contact information, marketing signals, and website structure, then organizes the data into structured reports. It also identifies strengths, weaknesses, and potential opportunities to help businesses make informed marketing and strategic decisions.

This project is being developed incrementally as part of a backend engineering and AI automation learning journey. Future versions will include AI-powered competitor summaries, business recommendations using local LLMs (Ollama), interactive dashboards, and automated report generation.
